# Deliverable 3 — Creative Direction (v0)

> **Status: v0.** Built from the locked brief + Stitch base hero. To be enriched after D2 (competitive) and D4 (moodboard) once research MCPs connect.

## One line
*A futuristic Japanese shrine for an AI/ML engineer's craft — calm, rare, and made by someone obsessive about detail.*

## What it is / isn't
- **Is:** editorial, restrained, content-led; sakura ground with matte ink; decoration that whispers; motion that means something.
- **Isn't:** a dev-portfolio template, a SaaS landing page, cyberpunk, anime, or a tuner car catalog. Remove anything generic, expected, or trend-chasing.

## Emotional target
Curiosity → intrigue → sophistication → trust. The visitor should feel they've entered a space that was *arranged*, not assembled.

## Locked identity (unchanged — confirmed sakura, not purple)
- Ground: sakura `#FFE4EC` / `#FFDCE7`; ink `#111`; accent crimson `#9D1B32` (<2% of surface).
- Narrow accents: steel, cyan, amber — only on small data surfaces.
- Type: SF Pro Display/Text (tight tracking), JetBrains Mono for data, brush serif (Shippori Mincho) for **kanji only**.
- Decoration: vertical low-opacity kanji; `零` brand glyph; desaturated wireframe car + blade silhouettes as *support*, never hero.

## Three design moves that carry the concept
1. **Negative space as the material.** Emptiness (Hara's *ma*) is the luxury signal — generous margins, one focal action per viewport.
2. **The single blade.** The section index reveals exactly one katana at a time (the shrine displays one artifact on its altar, not a rack). This is the memorable mechanic.
3. **Outcome-led artifacts.** Each project opens on its result, framed like a museum plaque; the case study is the deep exhibit behind it.

## Tone of voice
Honest student seeking AI/ML roles — confident, specific, measured. Numbers over adjectives. No senior-IC cosplay.
